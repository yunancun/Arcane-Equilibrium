# docker_projects

这里存放 Docker Compose 项目和服务部署文件。
This folder stores Docker Compose projects and service deployment files.

主要子目录说明：
Main subfolder overview:

- basic_system_services：基础服务，例如 PostgreSQL、Redis、向量数据库等。
- basic_system_services: core services such as PostgreSQL, Redis, and vector databases.

- monitoring_services：监控服务，例如 Grafana、Prometheus、Loki 和告警服务。
- monitoring_services: monitoring services such as Grafana, Prometheus, Loki, and alerting tools.

- market_data_services：行情抓取、新闻抓取和情报数据处理相关服务。
- market_data_services: services for market data collection, news collection, and intelligence processing.

- ai_agent_services：AI 分析、分类、判断和辅助决策相关服务。
- ai_agent_services: services related to AI analysis, classification, decision support, and review.

- trading_services：交易执行、订单管理和交易相关服务。
- trading_services: services related to trade execution, order management, and trading workflows.

- research_and_backtest_services：研究和回测相关服务。
- research_and_backtest_services: services related to research and backtesting.

- local_ai_services：本地 AI 服务，例如本地 embedding、本地模型推理和本地辅助 AI 服务。
- local_ai_services: local AI services such as local embeddings, local model inference, and local auxiliary AI services.

这个目录只放部署文件和服务定义，不放真实业务数据。
This folder should contain deployment files and service definitions only, not actual business data.
